Meet Your Trainer

Personal training became my passion 5 years ago. I started and still am training at Powerhouse Gym Family Fitness in Miller Place, NY. I love training and seeing people achieve their goals as I coach them through it. No goal is to dounting to achieve. I show my clients that they can shape their body however they desire.

I grew up in Setauket, NY and went to Ward Melville High School. After high school I went to Suffolk County Communtity College and recieved an Associates Degree in Libral Arts. During my time at Suffolk I discovered my love for training and nutrition and I now attend Dowling College located in Sherly, NY to get a Physical Education Teaching Degree.

If I am not training at the gym or doing work for my Masters Degree, I am hanging out with friends, hitting the beach, or playing softball.Baseball has always been my passion growing up and I am a die hard Mets Fan. I love watching football on sundays and monday nights are reserved for RAW.

Whether your goal is look good on the beach, drop 200 lbs, gain muscle, athletic fitness, or a healthy lifestyle, I will help get your body looking and feeling as good as possible. Lets put the hard work in together.
